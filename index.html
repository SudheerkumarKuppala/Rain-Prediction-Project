About the Project

SafeRouteX AI

This project predicts accident severity using XGBoost and identifies accident-prone areas using DBSCAN clustering. The system helps travelers understand accident risks based on weather, location, and temporal factors, providing actionable safety recommendations.

Python Flask XGBoost DBSCAN Scikit-Learn Pandas NumPy
Guide

How to Use SafeRouteX AI

Follow these simple steps to predict accident severity and get personalised travel recommendations.

01
Enter Location Details

Provide the start latitude, longitude, and the US state where you plan to travel.

02
Enter Weather & Date-Time

Fill in temperature, humidity, visibility, wind speed, pressure, and your planned travel date and hour.

03
Click Predict Severity

Hit the Predict Severity button and let the XGBoost model analyse your inputs instantly.

04
View Results & Recommendations

Review your predicted risk level, safety recommendations, and download a full PDF report.
